Welcome to Jedwall Design

Hey, my name is Jason and I am a passionate designer whether that is web design or graphic design, I really enjoy both worlds!

I have had +/- 10 years experience and am always looking to learn something new and work on new projects...

My favorite element of design is colour, one doesn't always need it but I really enjoy it.

If you want to get to know me more, drop me a line and we can chat over coffee, I will have alot of questions for you as well!
